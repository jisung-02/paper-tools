    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your file never leaves your device.

    What is preserved in the converted file?

    Only the extracted text is carried over, split into paragraphs — layout, images, tables and fonts from the original PDF are not preserved.

    Does this work on scanned PDFs?

    No — it extracts selectable text only; scanned or image-only PDFs have no embedded text to extract, since there is no OCR.
