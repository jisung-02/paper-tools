    How to flatten a PDF

    1. Drop a PDF file into the dropzone.
    2. Click "Flatten PDF".
    3. Download the PDF with visible annotations and form appearances converted to page content.

    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your file never leaves your device.

    What does flattening preserve?

    Anything visible — stamps, signatures and filled-in form fields — is drawn permanently into the page so it can no longer be edited or removed. Annotations with no visible content are dropped.
