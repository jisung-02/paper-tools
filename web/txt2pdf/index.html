    How to convert text to PDF

    1. Type or paste text, or drop a .txt file into the dropzone.
    2. Set the font size.
    3. Click "Create PDF" to download the PDF.

    FAQ

    Is my text uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your text never leaves your device.

    Is Korean text supported?

    Yes — an embedded NanumGothic font subset renders Korean (Hangul) text correctly in the PDF.
