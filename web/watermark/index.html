    How to add a watermark to a PDF

    1. Drop a PDF file into the dropzone.
    2. Enter the watermark text, and adjust size, opacity and diagonal placement.
    3. Click "Stamp" to download the watermarked PDF.

    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your file never leaves your device.

    Can I use Korean text in the watermark?

    Yes — a Korean-capable font is embedded into the PDF automatically, so Hangul renders correctly.
