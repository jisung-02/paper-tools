    How to rotate PDF pages

    1. Drop a PDF file into the dropzone.
    2. Choose an angle (90°, 180° or 270°) and, optionally, which pages to rotate (leave blank for all).
    3. Click "Rotate" to download the rotated PDF.

    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser via WebAssembly; your file never leaves your device.

    What page range formats are supported?

    Comma-separated numbers and dashed ranges, like "1,3-5". Leave the field blank to rotate every page.
