    How to remove PDF pages

    1. Drop a PDF file into the dropzone.
    2. Enter the pages to remove, e.g. "1-3,7".
    3. Click "Remove" to download the PDF with those pages deleted.

    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your file never leaves your device.

    What page range formats are supported?

    Comma-separated numbers and dashed ranges, like "1,3-5". Leave the end of a range blank, like "5-", to remove from that page to the end of the document.
