    How to print multiple pages per sheet (N-up)

    1. Drop a PDF file into the dropzone.
    2. Choose 2 per sheet (landscape A4) or 4 per sheet (portrait A4).
    3. Click "Combine" to download the N-up PDF.

    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser via WebAssembly; your file never leaves your device.

    Does N-up respect page rotation?

    No — pages are laid out 2 or 4 per A4 sheet, but any page rotation flags in the source PDF are ignored.
