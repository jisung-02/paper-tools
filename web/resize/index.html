    How to resize PDF pages

    1. Drop a PDF file into the dropzone.
    2. Choose a target size (A4 portrait, A4 landscape, Letter or A5) and, optionally, which pages to resize (leave blank for all).
    3. Click "Resize" to download the resized PDF.

    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your file never leaves your device.

    Are annotations preserved when resizing?

    No — links and annotations are removed, since the original page content is scaled to fit the new size.
