    FAQ

    Are my files uploaded to a server?

    No. The OCR engine and language data run and load entirely in your browser, from this site — your PDF or images never leave your device.

    How accurate is the text it finds?

    It works best on clean, high-resolution scans of printed text. Skewed or low-quality photos will have more errors, and handwriting recognition is hit-or-miss.

    Which languages are supported?

    English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified), Spanish, French and German. English also works reasonably well for many other languages that use the Latin alphabet.
