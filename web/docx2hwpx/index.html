    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser via WebAssembly; your file never leaves your device.

    What is preserved in the converted file?

    Only text and paragraphs are carried over; the resulting .hwpx is a minimal file that may render plainly when opened in Hancom Office.
