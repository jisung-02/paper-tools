FAQ

Is Paper Tools free?

Yes. All 34 tools are free, with no account and no signup.

Do my files get uploaded to a server?

No. Every tool runs entirely inside your browser; your files never leave your device.

Do I need to install anything?

No. It works in any modern browser, on desktop or mobile.

What can it do?

Merge, split, rotate, crop, compress, protect and unlock PDFs; convert images (PNG/JPG/GIF); convert Word/Hangul documents to PDF; extract text and images; and more.

Is it open source?

Yes — the source code is publicly available.
