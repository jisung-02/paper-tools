    How to insert a blank page

    1. Drop a PDF file into the dropzone.
    2. Set "After page" (0 inserts at the very front) and "Number of pages" to add.
    3. Click "Insert" to download the PDF with the blank pages added.

    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your file never leaves your device.

    What size are the inserted blank pages?

    Each inserted page is A4-sized, regardless of the size of the surrounding pages.
