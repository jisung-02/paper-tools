    How to permanently redact a PDF

    1. Drop in a PDF, then drag over each area you want to black out, on every page that needs it.
    2. Confirm the raster-only output warning once you're happy with the selections.

    FAQ

    Is the redacted content really gone, not just covered up?

    Yes — each page is rebuilt as a fresh image, so the original text, links and hidden objects underneath your selections are gone, not just painted over.

    Why is the output a raster (image-based) PDF?

    Rebuilding every page from pixels is what guarantees no trace of the removed content, selectable text or metadata survives — the tradeoff is a larger, non-selectable-text file.

    Is my PDF uploaded anywhere?

    No — the whole process, including rasterizing and rebuilding the PDF, happens locally in your browser.
