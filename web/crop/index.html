    How to crop PDF margins

    1. Drop a PDF file into the dropzone.
    2. Enter the margin to trim from each side in millimeters (left, bottom, right, top).
    3. Click "Crop" to download the trimmed PDF.

    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your file never leaves your device.

    Does cropping affect links or annotations?

    Yes — links and annotations are removed from the cropped PDF.
