      How to add a stamp or signature to a PDF

      1. Drop the PDF file into the "PDF file" dropzone.
      2. Choose image mode for a PNG/JPG stamp, or text mode for a name, date, note, or check mark.
      3. Click "Stamp" to download the stamped PDF.

      FAQ

      Are my files uploaded to a server?

      No — everything runs locally in your browser; your files never leave your device.

      Can the stamp image have a transparent background?

      Yes — a PNG image's transparency is preserved, so a signature or stamp with a transparent background overlays cleanly onto the page.

      Can I place typed text or a date instead of an image?

      Yes — use text mode to place a name, date, approval note, or check mark at the selected position.
