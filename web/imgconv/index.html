    How to convert an image

    1. Drop a PNG, JPG or GIF image into the dropzone.
    2. Choose the target format and, for JPG output, adjust the JPG quality.
    3. Click "Convert" to download the converted image.

    FAQ

    Is my image uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser via WebAssembly; your image never leaves your device.

    Which formats are supported?

    PNG, JPG and GIF only. The quality slider only affects JPG output.
