    How to add page numbers to a PDF

    1. Drop a PDF file into the dropzone.
    2. Choose a format ("n / N" or "n") and a font size.
    3. Click "Add Numbers" to download the numbered PDF.

    FAQ

    Is my file uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser via WebAssembly; your file never leaves your device.

    Where are the page numbers placed?

    At the bottom center of every page, either as "n / N" (page number and total) or just "n".
