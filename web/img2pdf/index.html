    How to turn images into a PDF

    1. Drop PNG or JPG images into the dropzone.
    2. Choose the PDF page size, orientation, image fit, margin, and EXIF rotation options.
    3. Click "Create PDF" to download one PDF with one image per page.

    FAQ

    Are my images uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your images never leave your device.

    Which image formats are supported?

    PNG and JPG. Each image becomes its own page, in the order you add the files.

    Can I add margins or use Letter pages?

    Yes. Choose A4 or Letter, then set margins and how each image should fit the page.

    Does JPEG rotation metadata work?

    Yes. Keep auto-rotate enabled to honor common EXIF orientation values without uploading your images.
