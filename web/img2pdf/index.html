    How to turn images into a PDF

    1. Drop one or more PNG or JPG images into the dropzone.
    2. Optionally check "Fit every page to A4" (off keeps each page at the image's original size).
    3. Click "Create PDF" to download a PDF with one image per page.

    FAQ

    Are my images uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser; your images never leave your device.

    Which image formats are supported?

    PNG and JPG. Each image becomes its own page, in the order you add the files.
