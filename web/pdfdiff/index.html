      How to compare two PDFs

      1. Drop the original PDF into the "A (original)" dropzone.
      2. Drop the revised PDF into the "B (revised)" dropzone.
      3. Click "Compare" to download a text diff report.

      FAQ

      Are my files uploaded to a server?

      No — everything runs locally in your browser; your files never leave your device.

      Does this compare the visual layout of the PDFs?

      Yes. Visual comparison renders pages locally and reports pixel, image, formatting, page-size and layout differences.
