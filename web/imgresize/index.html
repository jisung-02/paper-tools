    How to resize an image

    1. Drop a PNG, JPG or GIF image into the dropzone.
    2. Set a max width and/or max height in pixels (at least one is required), and a JPEG quality if the image is a JPG.
    3. Click "Resize" to download the resized image.

    FAQ

    Are my images uploaded to a server?

    No — everything runs locally in your browser via WebAssembly; your image never leaves your device.

    Does this ever enlarge my image?

    No — Image Resize only shrinks images that exceed the width/height you set; it never upscales a smaller image.
